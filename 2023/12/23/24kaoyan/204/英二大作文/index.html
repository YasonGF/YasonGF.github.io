静态化设计

Protecting environment is important.

important的同一替换: significant, essential, indispensable, play … role in,

我们可以对该句进行静态化处理

Protecting environment has great importance.

Protecting environment is of great/enormous importance.

（避免be动词裸奔，可以这样处理 do => try to do, be => tend to be）

Protecting environment tends to be of great importance.

英文是静态的语言，多用名词和介词

中文是动态的， 多用动词

静态化处理之动词名词化

sb intend(s) to do sth. => sb has/have intention of doing sth.

静态化处理之介词化

e.g. 我今天很开心！

I am very happy today.

I am in good mood today. (be+介词短语)

描述图画

引出图画：
  1. 未定式

As is vividly portrayed in the ridiculous picture, …

vividly (subtly, symbolically, clearly, ironically …)

portrayed(described, revealed, disclosed, shown, illustrated, …)

ridiculous(interesting, funny, thought-provoking, eye-catching, enlightening …)

picture (cartoon, photo, drawing, graph, …)

  1. 宾从法

the ridiculous painting vividly portrays that …

  1. 形式主语法

It is vividly portrayed in the ridiculous picture that …

当存在两幅图时， 可以使用similarly, likewise, furthermore, moreover, … 或者however, by contrast, unfortunately, yet, …

具体描述：

要求是： 结构多样性+用词多样性

组合和简化 ： 对简单句进行组合为复合句， 复合句再次进行简化。

将下面六个简单句组合为一个复合句

Last week I went to the theatre.

I had a very good seat.

The play was very interesting.

I did not enjoy it.

A young man and a young woman were sitting behind me.

They were talking loudly.

​

定从的简化： 当关系代词在定从中作主语时， 关系代词省略， 变定从谓语动词。

状从的简化：当状从的主语和主干的主语相同时， 确定是否保留引导词，将状从主语省略，变状从谓语动词。

独立主格结构 ：

Marry stood under a tree. Her dog sat at her feet.

Marry stood under a tree, with her dog sitting at her feet.

With her dog sitting at her feet, Marry stood under a tree.

Marry stood under a tree, her dog sitting at her feet.

通过上述的组合与简化知识，我们运用其能力来描述图画

e.g.1 旅途之”余“

a lake， rubbish， a boat， two tourists， throw rubbish

造简单句：

The lake is full of rubbish.

Two tourists are sitting on a boat.

They are throwing rubbish into the lake.

将其组合：

Two tourists, who are sitting on a boat, are throwing rubbish into the lake which is full of rubbish.

简化：

Two tourists sitting on a boat, are throwing rubbish into the lake full of rubbish.

添加修饰成分+词的不同表示：

From the picture, we can see that two tourists sitting on a boat, are throwing rubbish into the lake full of litter and waste.

e.g.2 与其只提要求，不如做个榜样

In the left picture, a father is watching TV.

The father is asking his son to study hard.

In the right picture, the father and the son are studying together.

组合:

The father who is watching TV is asking his son to study hard in the left picture.

While the father is watching TV in the picture, the father is asking his son to study hard.

In the right picture, the father and the son are studying together.

变为一句话：

As is vividly shown in the left picture, a father watching TV is asking his son to study hard, but both of the father and the son are studying together in the right picture.

While watching TV in the picture, a father is asking his son to study hard; however, both the father and the son are studying together in the right picture.

e.g.3 文化火锅，既美味又营养

culture，hotpot，delicious，trophic

There is a hot pot in the picutre.

There are some cultural elements in the hotpot.

The cultural elements include western cultural elements and Chinese cultural elements.

In the picture, there is a hot pot in which there are some cultural elements which include western cultural elements and Chinese cultural elements.

In the picture, there is a hot pot in which there are some cultural elements including western cultural ones and Chinese cultural ones.

As is symbolically depicted in the picture, there is a hot pot in which there are some cultural elements including western ones as well as Chinese ones.

描述图画救命方法：

The main information in the picture is that there be + (图片主要人\事物) + （doing\done …），which can be an obvious expression of the topic.

picture: drawing, painting, cartoon

obvious: vivid, apparent, actual, veritable, exact

expression: embodiment

topic: theme

The main information in the painting is that there is a flower put into different places, which can be a vivid embodiment of the topic.

The main information in the drawing is that there is an old man and a woman talking about a dragon boating race, which can be a apparent expression of the theme.

The main information in the cartoon is that there are two men climbing a mountain, which can be an actual embodiment of the topic.
